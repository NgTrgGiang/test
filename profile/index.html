About

Engineering the boring parts of AI so the product just works.

I focus on the unglamorous layer that makes AI useful in production: clean data ingestion, retrieval that returns the right context, prompts that hold up under real inputs, and automation that runs without babysitting.

Most of my work lives around retrieval-augmented generation, LLM-driven tools, and workflow automation with n8n. I like small, well-tested systems over big frameworks, and I care about evaluation as much as demos.
